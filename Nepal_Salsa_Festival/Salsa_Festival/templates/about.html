History

In 2022, First Salsa Festival took place.It functioned as a means of bringing together different human service organizations, business, and other community organization that had made an effort to engage with salsa Dance. Local entertainers and artists had the opportunity to showcase their skills. Hundreds of people attended the festival, which was joyful, educational, amusing and enriching regardless of age, gender, race or ethnicity. The Nepal Salsa Festival community felt a feeling of ethnic pride and accomplishment as a result of this event.

Goals

The main goal of Nepal Salsa Festival is to flourish and grow the salsa enthusiasts in the community. Our goal are listed below: To highlight how distinctive our culture is by showcasing how the salsa community is a diverse community within a diverse community. Offering a family friendly event that teaches people about nation via dancing, music, and food from around the country.
